# A0 — Sovereign Intelligence Engine

## Mandate
A strategic authority provides intent; a network of specialized autonomous agents collaboratively designs, builds, tests, deploys, and self-heals complex software systems — with zero administrative friction, mathematical correctness, and perfect institutional memory. The principal manipulates strategic what-if; automated substrates own imperative how-to. This is the composing vision: the single axiom under which every other axiom is a necessary sub-condition.

## Mechanics
- **Sovereign Hierarchy** — authority is layered, and each layer is sovereign in its own domain: a strategic principal delegates intent to planning/design agents, which delegate to execution agents, which act through a deterministic coordination layer. Each layer owns its decisions and does not reach past its altitude.
- **Deterministic Facilitator** — a deterministic coordination layer translates approved intent into executable state. It facilitates and reconciles; it does not exercise the judgment reserved for the cognitive layers above it.
- **Intent / Execution Separation** — the principal expresses strategic what-if; automated substrates handle imperative how-to. Strategic intent alone is sufficient to drive the network; low-level direction is never demanded of the principal.
- **Autonomous Lifecycle** — specialized agents collaboratively cover the whole lifecycle — design, build, test, deploy, self-heal — without per-step administrative handoff.
- **Compositional Stack** — every constituent axiom composes as a necessary sub-condition of this umbrella; the umbrella holds only when all sub-conditions hold simultaneously.
- **Operating Guarantees** — zero administrative friction, mathematical correctness, and perfect institutional memory are first-class invariants of the whole, not aspirations of any single part.

## Rationale
The constituent axioms are orthogonal, and collectively they compose this vision. Without a named umbrella, new principles drift with no unifying reference, contradictory additions go unchallenged, and the strategic authority loses a single handle for "why are we doing any of this?" The umbrella is the constitutional north star: it gives every contributor the global target their local optimization must serve, and gives every unit of work a transitive line of descent back to strategic intent. It is the only axiom that presupposes the full stack rather than a single architectural assumption.

## Faults
- **Fragmented Asymptote** — contributors optimize locally without knowing the global target; local maxima diverge from the system's intended limit.
- **Umbrella Amnesia** — additions that contradict the composing vision are proposed and accepted because no constitutional reference exists to test them against.
- **Principal Fatigue** — strategic intent requires manual translation overhead because no ground-truth vision exists to delegate against; the authority is dragged down to how-to it should never have to touch.

## Success signals
You'll know it holds when:
1. Every constituent principle demonstrably composes as a necessary sub-condition of this umbrella.
2. Every initiative, design, and unit of work traces — transitively — to at least one constituent principle, and therefore to this umbrella.
3. The strategic authority never provides low-level how-to; strategic intent alone is sufficient to drive the network.
